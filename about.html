About Us

Welcome to Pioneers University of Science & Technology.

Pioneers University of Science & Technology is one of the World's Top 100 Ranking Universities in 2023 by Times Higher Education.

History of PUST

Pioneers University of Science and Technology was founded in the year 2010, by visionary educators and philanthropists seeking to establish a center of learning that would foster Innovation, Research and Intellectual Growth.

Another aim was to make Science, Technology, Mathematics Education accessible to all from different walks of life.

As they are the drivers and forces of economic development and quality human welfare.

Situated in Ealing, West London, Pioneers University of Science & Technology began with the Faculty of Engineering & Applied Sciences, and just 20 students.

Growth And Expansion

The University for the last 15 years has undergone a considerable expansion. It now boasts of two more Faculties namely;

The Faculty of Health & Allied Sciences and the Faculty of Applied Sciences which was part of the Faculty of Engineering.

The University has also added up its number of campuses with the PSU Washington Campus in the United States of America and PSU Accra Campus, in West Africa, Ghana making it a Global University. These Campuses were established in 2018 and 2020 respectively.

It also boasts of a student body of approximately 15,000 globally and remains committed to its core mandate or mission of raising, preparing, training Game changers, Generational Thinkers, Leaders & Shapers.

Achievements

PUST has distinguished itself globally through academic excellence, innovation, and student success. Some of our major achievements include:

  • Ranked among the Top 100 Universities in the World (2023) by Times Higher Education for Employability Rate, Industry Income, and Accommodation facilities.
  • Recognized for pioneering cutting-edge research in Artificial Intelligence, Renewable Energy, and Biomedical Engineering.
  • Winner of multiple National Innovation Awards for projects led by students and faculty.
  • Partnerships with over 50 international universities for student exchange and collaborative research programs.
  • Consistently producing graduates with one of the highest employment rates in Europe and Africa.
  • Home to over 15,000 students globally, representing more than 30 nationalities, fostering diversity and inclusivity.

Through these milestones, PUST continues to uphold its mission of raising global leaders, innovators, and changemakers who impact society positively.
