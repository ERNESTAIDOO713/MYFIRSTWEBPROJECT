Move to the Next Level of your Academic Journey with PUST. Please the Follow the Steps below, to Apply.

Application Process

  • Access the Admissions Portal on the University's Website: www.PUSTApply.com NB: Students in Accra, Ghana are to purchase an E-voucher at any GCB and ECOBANK Branch Nationwide for 150 ghana cedis.
  • Prepare All Relevant Documents: Gather Academic Transcripts, Personal Statements, Letters of Recommendation, Results Slips, Passport Sized Photographs(3) for all category of Programmes and as well other Relevant Documents.
  • Choose Your Programme: Select Your Preferred Diploma, Undergraduate, Postgraduate or Doctorate Degree Programme.
  • Fill Out the Application Form: Fill out the Application Form provided. Make sure to provide accurate and relevant details or information. The University shall not be held liable for incorrect information provided.
  • Submit Your Application Details: Click the Submit Button and follow any instructions provided.
  • Track Your Application: Successful Applicants will recieve a confirmation email with login details.
  • Accept Your Offer: Once accepted, Confirm your enrolment to secure a spot.

Application Deadlines

  • Fall Semester:August 1.
  • Spring emester:December 1.
  • Summer Programmes:May 1.
  • Online Programmes:May 20.
