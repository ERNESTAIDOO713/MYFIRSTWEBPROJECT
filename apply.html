Apply Now

Move to the next level of your academic journey with PUST. Please follow the steps below, to apply.


Application Process

  • Access the Admissions Portal on the University's Website: www.pustapply.com
    NB: Students in Accra, Ghana are to purchase an E-voucher at any GCB and ECOBANK Branch Nationwide for 150 Ghana cedis.
  • Prepare All Relevant Documents: Gather Academic Transcripts, Personal Statements, Letters of Recommendation, Results Slips, Passport Sized Photographs (3), and other relevant documents.
  • Choose Your Programme: Select your preferred Diploma, Undergraduate, Postgraduate, or Doctorate Degree Programme.
  • Fill Out the Application Form: Ensure accurate and relevant information is provided. The university shall not be held liable for incorrect data.
  • Submit Your Application Details: Click the submit button and follow any instructions provided.
  • Track Your Application: Successful applicants will receive a confirmation email with login details.
  • Accept Your Offer: Once accepted, confirm your enrollment to secure a spot.

Application Deadlines

  • Fall Semester: August 1
  • Spring Semester: December 1
  • Summer Programmes: May 1
  • Online Programmes: May 20
